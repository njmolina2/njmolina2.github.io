Spaghetti with Meat Sauce

History of Spaghetti

Pasta/Noodles originated from the Mediterranean. It is believed that the Arabs introduced pasta to Europe during a conquest of Sicily.
Spaghetti comes from the Italian word "spaghetto", which means "little cord".

Spaghetti Ingredients

  • 12 ounces dried spaghetti
  • Large pot of water

Spaghetti Recipe

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil
  2. Put your 12 ounces of dried spaghetti into the pot
  3. Cook the spaghetti according to its package directions
  4. check for doneness 1-2 minutes before recommended finishing time
  5. Strain spaghetti from the water

Meat Sauce Ingredients

  • 1 pound of ground beef
  • 1 pound of ground pork
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 cup diced onion
  • 3 minced garlic cloved
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • 1 jar/can of tomato sauce

Meat Sauce Recipe

  1. Heat the olive oil in a pan over medium-high heat
  2. Add in the meat(s) of your choice
  3. Cook until browned
  4. using a wooden spoon/spatchula, break the meat down into smaller chunks
  5. Once browned, add in your onion, garlic, salt, and pepper
  6. Add in your jar of tomato sauce
  7. Let simmer
  8. Once finished, add meat sauce to the spaghetti and stir

Optional - Add parmesan cheese as a finishing touch
